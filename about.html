About Edwards Group Holdings

A trusted media company with deep roots in local communities, dedicated to serving South Carolina, Wyoming, and Michigan through quality journalism, engaging radio programming, and professional printing services.

Our Story

Edwards Group Holdings represents decades of commitment to community journalism and media excellence. Founded with the vision of connecting communities through trusted communication, we are a multi-state media company serving diverse markets across three states.

Our journey began with a simple belief: that local communities deserve quality journalism, engaging entertainment, and professional services that reflect their values and serve their needs. This principle continues to guide us as we expand our reach while maintaining our commitment to local excellence.

Today, Edwards Group Holdings operates newspapers, radio stations, and printing companies that have become integral parts of their communities. We take pride in being more than just media companies: we are community partners, local employers, and trusted voices in the markets we serve.
