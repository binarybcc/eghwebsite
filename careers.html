Internships & Entry-Level Opportunities

Start Your Media Career

Edwards Group Holdings offers internship and entry-level opportunities for students and recent graduates looking to start their careers in media, journalism, broadcasting, or related fields.

Internship Benefits

  • Hands-on experience in real media operations
  • Mentorship from experienced professionals
  • Exposure to multiple aspects of media business
  • Networking opportunities within the industry
  • Potential for full-time employment opportunities
  • College credit available for qualifying programs

Internship Areas

  • Editorial and Reporting
  • Radio Production and Broadcasting
  • Graphic Design and Layout
  • Digital Marketing and Social Media
  • Sales and Business Development
  • Photography and Multimedia

Apply for Internships

We accept internship applications year-round and work with students to accommodate academic schedules and requirements.

Application Requirements

  • Resume and cover letter
  • Portfolio or writing samples (for relevant positions)
  • Letter of recommendation
  • Academic transcript
  • Statement of learning objectives
