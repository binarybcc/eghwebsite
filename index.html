About Edwards Group Holdings

Edwards Group Holdings is a multi-state media company committed to serving communities through quality journalism, engaging radio programming, and professional printing services. Based in Seneca, South Carolina, our company operates across three states, connecting with local audiences through trusted brands and community-focused content.

Our mission is to inform, entertain, and serve the communities we call home. From local news coverage to commercial printing solutions, we take pride in being an integral part of the communities we serve.
