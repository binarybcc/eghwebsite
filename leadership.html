Leadership Team

Our experienced leadership team brings decades of media industry expertise, business acumen, and community commitment to guide Edwards Group Holdings' continued growth and success.

Executive Leadership

Our Leadership Philosophy

Guiding Principles

Our leadership team is committed to fostering a culture of excellence, innovation, and community service. We believe in leading by example and empowering our employees to deliver exceptional results for our communities and stakeholders.

Leadership Values

  • Community Focus: Every decision considers the impact on our local communities
  • Employee Development: Investing in our team's growth and professional development
  • Innovation: Embracing new technologies and methods while honoring tradition
  • Integrity: Maintaining the highest ethical standards in all business practices
  • Collaboration: Working together across divisions to achieve common goals
  • Accountability: Taking responsibility for our actions and results

Combined Experience

Our leadership team brings over 100 years of combined experience in media, business operations, and community service.

Local Expertise

Deep understanding of the unique needs and opportunities in each of our three-state markets.

Industry Knowledge

Comprehensive expertise across publishing, radio broadcasting, and commercial printing operations.

Organizational Structure

How We Operate

Edwards Group Holdings operates through a collaborative leadership structure that balances corporate oversight with local autonomy. Our approach allows each division and market to maintain its unique character while benefiting from shared resources and expertise.

Benefits of Our Structure

  • Local decision-making authority for market-specific needs
  • Shared resources and best practices across divisions
  • Coordinated strategic planning and growth initiatives
  • Consistent quality standards and professional development
  • Efficient operations through centralized support services
